Who We Are

Our Identity & Purpose

Our Mission

To foster an inclusive environment where every community member — regardless of background, gender, or circumstance — can access the tools, networks, and support needed to realise their full potential and contribute meaningfully to a thriving society.

Our Vision

A self-reliant, just, and compassionate Kenya where communities lead their own development agendas — where every star shines at its brightest, and no individual is left without a voice, an opportunity, or a path to dignity.

The Pillars We Stand On

Seven Core Values

  1. 01

    Integrity

    We hold ourselves to the highest standards of transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct in all operations.

  2. 02

    Ubuntu

    "I am because we are." Our work is grounded in African humanist philosophy — collective dignity, shared humanity.

  3. 03

    Inclusivity

    We celebrate diversity and ensure every programme, space, and opportunity is accessible to all — no exceptions.

  4. 04

    Sustainability

    Our initiatives are designed for lasting impact — environmentally conscious, financially prudent, and socially rooted.

  5. 05

    Empowerment

    We transfer knowledge, resources, and agency to community members — building capacity from within, not dependency.

  6. 06

    Collaboration

    Partnerships amplify impact. We work alongside government, civil society, businesses, and the people we serve.

  7. 07

    Community Engagement

    The community is both the author and beneficiary of change. Active, meaningful participation guides every decision.
